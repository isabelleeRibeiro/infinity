New Release

Comfort Access

I.I.I Infinity introduces to the market the Comfort Access, a vehicle designed to transform the mobility experience of people with disabilities (PWD), especially those who use wheelchairs. More than just an automobile, this release symbolizes the union between innovation, accessibility, and respect for individual autonomy.

Description

Comfort Access

Designed with an integrated side access ramp and a spacious interior, the Comfort Access was developed to ensure not only practicality but also comfort, safety, and dignity in every journey. Its modern and functional design reflects I.I.I Infinity’s commitment to solutions that meet real needs while maintaining aesthetics and sophistication.

Created for

Comfort Access

Among the main audiences considered are wheelchair users, whose conditions may vary depending on the type and degree of disability. These include:
Congenital motor disabilities: such as cerebral palsy or congenital malformations that impair mobility.
Disabilities acquired through accidents: cases of spinal cord injuries resulting in paraplegia or quadriplegia...

Description

Comfort Access

The Comfort Access was designed to meet this diversity of profiles, offering facilitated boarding, interior space adapted for different types of wheelchairs, and ergonomic seats that provide safety for both driver and passengers.
More than accessibility, this model offers inclusion: it represents the possibility of living independently, carrying out daily activities with greater freedom, and redefining urban mobility for the PWD community.

Purpose

Comfort Access

With Comfort Access, I.I.I Infinity reaffirms its mission to develop visionary solutions that combine technology, comfort, and humanity. This vehicle is not just a means of transportation, but a true ally in building a more just, accessible, and inclusive society.
